Typical triggers

Revenue rises, margins stay flat - hidden friction is eroding profit.

Data is present, but not trusted - teams correct reality after the fact.

1. Confirm reality

What is happening - not what is claimed

I validate how work actually flows, where decisions are made, and how data is produced.

2. Locate control loss

Where cost is created silently

Bottlenecks, delayed signals, rework loops, and “interpretation zones” where rules should exist.

3. Decision path

What to fix next - and how

Structural redesign, clarified ownership, enforcement rules, or system-level validation.
